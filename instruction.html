In this game you will guess the random word that is given to you everytime you retry the game and also whenever you login again or you gameover.
There are 3 different colors inside the game: Green , Orange and Gray.
Green means that the letter of the word you are trying to guess is correct and is in the word.
Orange means that that it is present in the word.
Gray means its absent or not in the word.
You get 6 guesses and the right word only consist of 5 letters.
In order to win you need to lookout for green letters
